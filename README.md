# SWARM

A pheromone puzzle game. Place beehives on a dark map scattered with flowers, then watch hundreds of bees solve the foraging problem through emergent collective intelligence.

**[Play SWARM](https://adamhede.github.io/pheromone/swarm/)**

## How to Play

1. Click to place your hive(s) on the map
2. Press **GO** (or spacebar) to release the swarm
3. Watch bees explore, discover food, and build glowing pheromone highways
4. Score is based on food collected — earn up to 3 stars per map

## The Simulation

Bees leave the hive with no plan. They wander, leaving faint blue exploration trails. When a bee finds food, she returns home along her trail, now laying bright golden recruitment pheromone. Other bees sense it and follow. Paths reinforce. Highways form. The map comes alive with luminous, branching rivers of light.

Your job: read the map, place your hives wisely, and watch your swarm paint the darkness with light.

## Maps

- **First Flight** — Open field, one cluster
- **Fork** — Two clusters, watch the swarm split
- **The Wall** — Barrier with a gap
- **Garden** — Scattered flowers, two hives
- **Maze** — Navigate corridors
- **The Choice** — Near and small, or far and rich?
- **Freeplay** — Procedurally generated, infinite replayability
